CORPORATE OUTREACH
We Form Strategic Alliances
As a Corporate Outreach organizer, you will be responsible for contacting companies for financial sponsorship, hardware, swag, and mentorship. We build relationships with existing partners and reach out to other local companies to support HackUCI. Help us communicate with tech companies by using the following strategies: diligent emailing, scheduling phone calls, attending career fairs, participating at hackathons, and networking at professional events! Help us grow our professional network so that we can provide better experiences for our event attendees.
